Upload Guide

Found a call worth arguing about? Here's how to get it on the board in under a minute.

1

Find the clip on YouTube

Track down the match on YouTube and play it up to the disputed moment. PSA, amateur, league — if the call is contested, it belongs here.

2

Copy the video URL

Grab the full link, e.g. youtube.com/watch?v=…. Paste it into the submit form.

3

Note the timestamp

Mark when the call happens — 4:20 or 1:06:09. We play a 20-second window from there so voters see exactly what you saw.

4

Submit it

Drop in the URL and timestamp. Email is optional — leave it if you want a heads-up when your clip goes live.
