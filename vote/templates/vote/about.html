Why I created SquashVote.wtf?

As a squash player, I know how frustrating it can be when a referee makes a terrible call. I love watching PSA matches, but even at the highest level, a call can leave you flabbergasted. Now that squash is headed to the Olympics, it's more important than ever to shine a light on referee standards. So I decided to create a platform where squash fans like myself can vote on what you think the correct decision should be.
